Covid-19 Trivia


  • COVID-19, a name known to all, is an abbreviation of Coronavirus Disease. It is caused by a newly discovered coronavirus part of the family - Coronaviridae.
  • The COVID-19 virus mainly spreads through droplets of saliva or discharge from the nose when a person coughs or sneezes.
  • COVID-19 infections give rise to mild symptoms in the majority of the cases and do not require any special treatment. Older people and persons with co-morbidities are more vulnerable and may develop serious illnesses.
  • Some of the most common symptoms include -
    • Fever or chills
    • Cough
    • Shortness of breath or difficulty breathing
    • Fatigue
    • Muscle or body aches
    • Headache
    • New loss of taste or smell
    • Sore throat
    • Congestion or runny nose
    • Nausea or vomiting
    • Diarrhea
  • Covid-19 Origins:
  • To this day, scientists are trying to cotemplate the origins of the virus. The two prevailing competing theories are that the virus jumped from animals, possibly originating with bats, to humans, or that it escaped from a virology laboratory in Wuhan, China.
