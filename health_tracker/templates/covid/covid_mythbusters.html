Covid-19 Mythbusters


  • MYTH - Alcohol based sanitisers are unsafe and cannot be used often.
  • FACT - Alcohol based sanitisers are safe for everyone to use and do not create any relevant health issues. These sanitisers can be used often as they don't pose any side-effects nor do they lead to antibiotic resistance.
  • MYTH - Vitamins and mineral supplements(capsules) can cure COVID-19.
  • FACT - Vitamins and mineral supplements cannot cure COVID-19. However, they can help boost your immunity.
  • MYTH - One should always wear masks even during exercising.
  • FACT - One should not wear a mask while exercising since it reduces air circulation and can lead to oxygen deficiency. Sweat makes the mask wet and it can become the site of microbial growth.
  • MYTH - Thermal scanners can be used to detect COVID-19.
  • FACT - Thermal scanners cannot detect COVID-19 and can only detect temperature. It can be used to detect fevers.
  • MYTH - A pneumonia vaccine is sufficient to prevent COVID-19 infection.
  • FACT - Vaccines against pneumonia will not protect you against COVID-19 virus.
  • MYTH - Antibiotics can be used to treat COVID-19 infections and symptoms.
  • FACT - Antibiotics are not effective against COVID-19 virus. Antibiotics are only effective against bacterial infections.
